Is this 1:1 coaching?

No. It is a guided behavioural programme with cohort accountability, founder-led group touchpoints, and a small Pattern Pack of peers running the same pattern. 1:1 is available separately for senior leaders.

Who is this not for?

Generic self-help seekers, students, anyone looking for a £29 habit tracker, or anyone who wants insight without doing real-world reps.

What if I miss a day?

Programmes are designed to absorb missed days. The system tracks pattern frequency, not perfection. Two missed days do not reset the climb.

How is this different from Headspace, BetterUp, or coaching apps?

Those teach. We train. Every week you complete a witnessed Golden Challenge in your real environment. You do not consume content. You build proof.
